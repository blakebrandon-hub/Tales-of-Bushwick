# Tales of Bushwick

An AI-powered text adventure about surviving New York City one bad decision at a time. You're late for an interview in Dumbo, your roommate Skylar has been in the bathroom for forty minutes, and your phone is at 14%. Every choice costs you dignity, cash, or both. The city is indifferent. The radiator is hissing. The L train has "signal problems." 

Powered by Claude, the narrative adapts to every decision you make — no two playthroughs are the same.
